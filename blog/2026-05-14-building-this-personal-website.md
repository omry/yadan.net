This first post is about making `yadan.net` into a small personal website:
enough structure for projects, blog, about, and contact, without turning it
into a full resume or a busy homepage.

<!-- truncate -->

This is not my first blog. I still have an unmaintained
[Hebrew blog](https://firefang.net/blog/) at `firefang.net`, where I posted between 2005 and 2016,
with the frequency slowly tapering off.

This one should be easier to sustain because it is not only a place to write.
It is also a small front door for the projects I keep returning to: Hydra,
OmegaConf, Rakia, HoodleFinance, and whatever comes next.

The goal is simple: keep a stable home for project context and occasional
writing, with contact available as a small standalone page, without turning the
homepage into a full resume or portfolio.
